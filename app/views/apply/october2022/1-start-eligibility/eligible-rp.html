You’re eligible to apply

Before you start, you'll need

  • your unique reference number - you may have received this in an email or text message
  • your National Insurance number
  • details of the children you're applying for
  • the other parent's first and last name and as much other information about them as you can give - for example, where they live or their phone number
  • your bank or building society account details, if you need to give them to the other parent

You can also speak to your bank or building society about setting up an account that will not give away your location.
