What is your unique reference number?

You may have received an email or text message with your unique reference number. It starts with 85, 95 or 96.

I need to pay child maintenance to more than one person

You need to complete a separate application using a different unique reference number. You can contact the Child Maintenance Service (opens in new tab) or apply for a unique reference number through Get Help Arranging Child Maintenance

I cannot apply online

You can contact the Child Maintenance Service to discuss your maintenance arrangements, get a unique reference number and find out how to apply.
