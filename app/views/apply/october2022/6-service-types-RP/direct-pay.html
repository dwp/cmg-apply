How you get child maintenance

There are two ways you can get child maintenance payments.

Direct Pay

The other parent will pay child maintenance directly into your bank account.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent and there are no additional fees.

Collect and Pay

The Child Maintenance Service will collect maintenance from the other parent and pass it to you.

There are extra fees for this service, which are:

  • 20% will be added to the amount the other parent pays
  • 4% will be taken off the payment you get

For example, if the payment amount was £100, the other parent would pay £120 and you would get £96.

You can only start with Collect and Pay if the other parent agrees to it.

Collect and Pay can be enforced if the other parent stops responding to us or stops making payments.
