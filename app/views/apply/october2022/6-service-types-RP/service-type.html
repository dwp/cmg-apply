How we decide your service type

We will decide your service type based on:

  • your service type preference
  • the other parent’s service type preference
  • the other parent’s payment history using the Child Maintenance Service - for example, if they have missed payments for another child maintenance case

We will contact you to let you know whether you’ll get payments by Direct Pay or Collect and Pay.
