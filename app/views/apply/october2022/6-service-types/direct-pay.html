How you pay child maintenance

Most separated parents who use the Child Maintenance Service start with a ‘Direct Pay’ arrangement. This is where you make payments direct to the other parent’s bank account. There are no fees if you use Direct Pay.

We will:

  • work out how much maintenance must be paid, and tell both parents
  • give both parents a payment schedule
  • review your calculation each year, or if either parent’s circumstances change

If you do not want contact with the other parent

If you ask us to, we can get the other parent’s bank details so you can make payments to their account.

If payments are not made

If you miss a Direct Pay payment, we can change your arrangement to Collect and Pay and take enforcement action to get unpaid child maintenance from you, such as:

  • take money directly from your earnings
  • take money from your bank account or building society
  • take action through the courts
