Application sent

You don't need to keep your unique reference number that we sent you any longer.

What happens next

The Child Maintenance Service will:

  • send your customer reference number in a text message (you will need this to sign in to your online account) 
  • try to contact the other parent 
  • confirm whether your service type will be Direct Pay or Collect and Pay
  • tell you how much child maintenance you should get and when you should get it

How long your application will take

It can take up to 6 weeks to process your application. Complex cases may take longer.

Updates about your application

You can track your application in your online child maintenance account (opens in new tab). We will email you if the status of your application changes. 

To sign in to your account, you'll need your:

  • customer reference number, it will start with 121 (we will send this to you within 5 days)  
  • 7-digit personal identification number (PIN)
  • National Insurance Number 

If we have any questions about your application, we will call you from this number:

0800 171 2345

You might want to add this number to your contact list so you know it’s us calling.

If you do not receive your customer reference number within 5 days, you can contact the Child Maintenance Service (opens in new tab).
