Find out how to tell us about expenses or additional income that could affect your child maintenance calculation

Additional expenses

Your child maintenance calculation may reduce if you are employed or self-employed and have additional expenses.

For example, if you spend over £10 a week on transportation to keep in regular contact with your children.

Additional income

Your child maintenance calculation may increase if you have another source of income, as well as your income from employment, self-employment, or benefits.

For example, if you earn over £2500 a year in rent from property or land.

How to tell us about additional expenses or income

Once you complete your application, you can use your online child maintenance account to report additional expenses or income. We will tell you how to set this up later in the application.
