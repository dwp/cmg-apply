Is Child Benefit part of Universal Credit?
No, Child Benefit and Universal Credit are different services - you can apply for either, or both, and you must apply for each one separately.

The child element of Universal Credit is not Child Benefit.

You do not need to receive Universal Credit to receive Child Benefit payments.
Can I receive Child Benefit for this child if I have more than 2 children?
Yes, there’s no limit to how many children you can claim Child Benefit payments for.
