How you make Collect and Pay payments

If we decide your service type will be Collect and Pay, there are 2 ways you can make payments. They are: 

  • Direct debit - this is where you send payments to the Child Maintenance Service and we pass them on to the other parent
  • Deduction of earnings - this is where we take your child maintenance payments directly from your wage

How would you prefer to make Collect and Pay payments?

Your child maintenance payments will be taken directly from your salary by your employer. If you have more than one employer, we may need to collect payments from all of them.

We’ll contact you to get the information we need to arrange the payments, and let you know when the payments will be taken.
