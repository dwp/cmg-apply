If you do not think Direct Pay will work

We can collect payments from you and pass them on to the other parent. This is called ‘Collect and Pay’. Both parents will be charged if you use Collect and Pay.

When you can use Collect and Pay

You can only choose to start with a Collect and Pay arrangement if the other parent agrees to it.

Collect and Pay can also be enforced if you:

  • stop paying using Direct Pay
  • do not respond to us

If you use Collect and Pay

We will:

  • work out how much the payment will be and tell both parents
  • collect payments from you and pass them on to the other parent – there are charges for this
  • review your calculation each year, or if either parent’s circumstances change

Charges for using Collect and Pay

You and the other parent will both be charged fees if you use Collect and Pay.

When you pay child maintenance, 20% is added to each payment. When the other parent gets paid child maintenance, 4% will be taken from each payment.

If the payment amount was £100, you would pay £120 and the other parent would get £96.
