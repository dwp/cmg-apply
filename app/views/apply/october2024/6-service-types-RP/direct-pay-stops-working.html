Getting payments by Direct Pay

We will:

  • work out how much child maintenance must be paid, and tell both parents
  • give both parents a payment schedule
  • review your calculation each year, or if either parent’s circumstances change

If payments are not made

If the other parent stops paying child maintenance using Direct Pay or stops responding to us, we can enforce Collect and Pay. There are additional fees for this.

We can also take action to get any missed payments, such as:

  • take money directly from the other parent’s earnings
  • take money from the other parent’s bank account or building society
  • take action through the courts
