You’re eligible to apply

Before you start, you'll need:

  • your unique reference number - you may have received this in an email or text message
  • your National Insurance number
  • information about your income or benefits
  • details of the children you’re applying for
  • the other parent's name and as much other information about them as you can give – for example, where they live or their phone number
  • details of any children you support who are not part of this arrangement – for example, a child that lives with you or a child you have with a different partner

After you apply:

We will try to contact the other parent to set up an arrangement. We cannot set up an arrangement if the other parent:

  • does not respond to us
  • chooses not to receive child maintenance payments

If the other parent accepts the child maintenance arrangement, we will send you a letter explaining how much you need to pay and when you need to pay it.
