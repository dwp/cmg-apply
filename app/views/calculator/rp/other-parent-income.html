What is the other parent's total taxable income?

This is their total income from employment or self-employment, before tax and National Insurance, but after pension contributions. This can be weekly, monthly or yearly. You can enter whole numbers, or numbers with one or two decimal places, for example, £100, £100.40 or £1000.40.
