If the other parent gets benefits 

If the other parent gets benefits or State Pension, the Child Maintenance Service calculates a 'Flat Rate' of £7 per week.

The "Flat Rate" is only used if:

  • the other parent does not have any other source of income
  • the other parent has another source of income but gets certain benefits
